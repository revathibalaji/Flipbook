 ABOUT US 
 

       
Fix Nix is an Information security company born on a vision to democratize the security 
landscape of the world. Fix Nix provides varied services in the security domain from a 
security audit to a Virtual Chief Information Security Officer (V CISO).

       We sensed there is a need for every Small and Medium Enterprise (SME) in the 
world today for an information security product, at least a data encryption and a data  
loss prevention module. Every Company in the ISMS and GRC(Governance, Risk Management, 
Compliance) space offers services and management systemsonly to the Fortune 
1000 companies of the world.
